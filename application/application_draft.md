**Q20 Describe the solution**  
The submitted solution is a trust layer embedded into a live fintech journey. It detects trust-sensitive moments such as fee disclosure, suspicious transaction behavior, consent for data use, complaint handling, repayment stress, and collections. It then presents customer-facing safeguards and records gender-disaggregated outcomes so product, risk, CX, and compliance teams can see whether women customers are safer, more confident, and more likely to keep using the service.

**Q21 What does it enable?**  
Improve transparency and consent practices; detect and prevent fraud or scams; improve complaint handling and resolution; reduce exclusion and bias in decisions; improve collections practices and oversight.

**Q22 Problem solved for women customers**  
Women customers often face lower confidence in digital finance, unclear fees, higher exposure to social-engineering scams, lower tolerance for hidden risk, and less visible complaint recourse. The trust layer reduces those barriers by making terms clear before commitment, warning against suspicious flows, documenting consent, escalating complaints, and reporting whether women customers actually experience better trust and usage outcomes.

**Q23 Existing approaches improved or replaced**  
It improves isolated fraud tools, generic disclosure screens, and after-the-fact complaint dashboards by connecting them into one measurable trust loop. Instead of treating trust as a brand claim, the system instruments customer moments, action taken, gender-disaggregated outcome, and operational follow-up.

**Q24 Customer journey digitally enabled**  
Onboarding, product discovery/application, transactions/payments, servicing/support, repayment/collections, and claims/issue resolution.

**Q25 Business objective or commercial success**  
Trust is treated as a growth and risk-control mechanism. By reducing confusion, scams, unresolved complaints, and harmful collections, the partner can improve activation, repeat usage, retention, referrals, and portfolio quality while strengthening consumer-protection posture.
