## Kill Shots

- Trust is measured as operational behavior, not described as brand sentiment.
- The solution connects women-specific barriers to product changes, event evidence, and business outcomes.
- GDD is used as a control loop: identify gap, change journey, measure outcome, escalate harm.
- Partner data gates are explicit, which prevents overclaiming before final submission.
