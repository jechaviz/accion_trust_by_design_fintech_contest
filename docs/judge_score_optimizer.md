## Minimum Partner Data To Beat Strong Entrants

- 10,000+ active users preferred, but lower can work if retention and trust lift are strong.
- Women customer share and active-user count.
- At least one 12-month before/after metric.
- One outcome that connects trust to behavior: retention, repayment, complaint resolution, fraud avoided, product uptake, or repeat transaction.
- One qualitative quote or interview summary from women customers.
- One operational change from GDD.

## Answer Pattern

Every long answer should follow:

1. Barrier faced by women customers.
2. Product change or safeguard.
3. Metric and evidence source.
4. Business impact.
5. Privacy/consent/harm-control note.
