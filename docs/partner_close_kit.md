# Partner Close Kit

## 15-Minute Partner Pitch

You already have the eligible fintech. We bring a near-finished Accion Trust by
Design submission package, trust layer demo, V metric engine, and Microsoft
Forms automation dry-run. Together, we can submit a credible trust-and-GDD
application before the deadline without overclaiming or exposing private data.

## Partner Value

- Chance at $50k grand prize or $10k finalist award.
- External recognition in trust-centered fintech.
- Reusable trust reporting layer for product, risk, CX, and compliance.
- Stronger narrative for investors and customer protection.

## What We Need In 24 Hours

- Authorized representative.
- Legal entity and contact facts.
- Active customer range and women customer share.
- One trust, retention, complaint, fraud, repayment, or engagement metric.
- One 12-month trend or before/after comparison.
- One GDD-informed product or policy change.
- Privacy/consent/complaint safeguards.

## Call Script

1. Confirm eligibility and authority.
2. Confirm women customers and GDD availability.
3. Identify strongest trust barrier: fraud, unclear terms, complaint recourse, consent, over-indebtedness, collections, bias.
4. Pick one metric with baseline and current value.
5. Decide what can be public, aggregate, or private.
6. Schedule final authorized submit session.
