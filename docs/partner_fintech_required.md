# Partner Fintech Required

The `student_partner_track` package is designed for a student team to partner
with an eligible fintech. The official applicant must be the fintech or a person
authorized to bind that fintech to the rules.

## Required Partner Profile

- Live digital financial-services product with active customers.
- Women customers are a current user group.
- Can export or report gender-disaggregated data for the submitted solution.
- Has evidence of traction: active users, repeat usage, retention, repayment, satisfaction, complaint resolution, fraud-loss reduction, or comparable measures.
- Has privacy, consent, complaint, and consumer-protection controls.
- Can describe regulatory posture in operating markets, including licensing model, KYC/AML, consumer protection, data protection, and partner dependencies.

## Partner Intake

Partner must provide:

- legal entity name and trade name;
- year founded;
- website;
- primary market and optional secondary markets;
- organization type;
- primary contact name, title, function, email, and phone;
- owner function for the submitted solution;
- deployment confirmation and active user range;
- women-customer share;
- annual revenue range;
- one 12-month traction indicator and before/after trend;
- GDD practice level;
- one concrete product or policy change from gender data;
- qualitative research or co-design evidence;
- safeguards against privacy, consent, bias, over-indebtedness, fraud, complaint, and collections harms.
